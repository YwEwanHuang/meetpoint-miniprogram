## Users

People who want to meet up in person - couples, friends, or family members who aren't at the same location and want to find the optimal meeting point. The app is designed for Chinese users on WeChat.

## Product Purpose

Two people pair anonymously via a 6-digit code, share their real-time locations on a map, and get an algorithmically calculated meeting point that minimizes travel time difference between both parties. No need to add each other as friends.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, reliable, Apple-like simplicity. The feel should be "this just works" - clean, minimal, with precise spacing and typography. Not playful or decorative. Think precision rather than personality.

## Anti-references

- No gaudy gradients or heavy shadows
- No cartoonish illustrations
- No overwhelming colors
- No busy layouts or cluttered cards
- No emoji as interface icons

## Strategic Design Principles

1. **Clarity above all** - Each page has one clear purpose. Navigation is intuitive.
2. **Trust through design** - Location sharing requires trust; the design should feel safe and reliable.
3. **Apple HIG compliance** - Follow iOS design patterns: large titles, grouped table sections, precise spacing (8pt grid), SF system fonts.
4. **State clarity** - Paired/unpaired states must be visually distinct. Empty states should guide action.
5. **Map is the hero** - On the map page, the map dominates. The bottom panel is a tool, not the main feature.
